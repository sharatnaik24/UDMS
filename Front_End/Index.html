About UDMS

A unique initiative, the University Details Management System (UDMS) is designed to streamline and integrate the operations and governance of a university or institution. This system centralizes data management within the institution, ensuring uniformity and efficiency in the administration of higher education. UDMS benefits students, faculty, and administrators by providing a unified platform for managing academic details, improving accessibility, and enhancing the overall educational experience.
